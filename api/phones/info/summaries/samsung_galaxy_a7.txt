The Samsung Galaxy A7 (2018) is a midrange Android smartphone produced by Samsung Electronics as part of the Samsung Galaxy A series. It was announced on 20 September 2018 as the successor to the Samsung Galaxy A7 (2017).

The A7 (2018) is the first triple camera smartphone produced by Samsung, featuring 3 different camera on the rear. It features a 6 inch Super AMOLED Infinity Display with curved edges similar to the Samsung Galaxy A8 (2018), a side mounted fingerprint sensor on the power button and Dolby Atmos immersive sound technology.
