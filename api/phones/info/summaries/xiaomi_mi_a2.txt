The Xiaomi Mi A2 is a re-branded Xiaomi Mi 6X phone, co-developed by Google—as part of its Android One initiative—and Xiaomi that runs on the Android operating system.

The phone features a 5.99 inches Full HD+ (1080 x 2160 pixels resolution) 403ppi pixel density display, unimetal body, and Corning Gorilla Glass 5 protection. It is powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon 660 processor and Adreno 512 GPU.and has a 2.0, Type-C 1.0 reversible connector. It has a dual rear camera (Primary camera is 12MP Sony IMX486 sensor of 1.25μm pixel size and f1.75 aperture and Secondary has 20MP Sony IMX376 sensor of 2.0μm pixel size f1.75 aperture). The front camera is 20MP same Sony IMX 376 sensor of 2.0μm pixel size with f2.2 aperture. The battery is of 3010 mAh which supports Qualcomm Quick charge 4.0+.
