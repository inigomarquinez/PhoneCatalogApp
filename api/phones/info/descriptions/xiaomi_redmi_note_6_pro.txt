The Redmi Note 6 Pro is the successor of the Redmi Note 5 Pro. It has a notched display that measures 6.26 inches and has Corning Gorilla Glass for protection. Powering the Redmi Note 6 Pro is a Qualcomm Snapdragon 636 processor which is an octa-core processor clocked at 1.8GHz. There is 6GB of RAM and 64GB of storage. It has a hybrid dual-SIM slot which you can use to expand storage. The Redmi Note 6 Pro has a dual camera setup at the back consisting of a 12-megapixel primary camera and a 5-megapixel depth sensor. At the front it has a dual selfie camera consisting of a 20-megapixel primary sensor and a 2-megapixel depth sensor. It has a 4000mAh battery and has support for Qualcomm Quick Charge 3.0.
